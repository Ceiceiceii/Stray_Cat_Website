What Will We Do? 🐈

As we enter the colder months, it's important to remember our furry friends who are left to brave the harsh winter conditions on the streets. Stray cats are some of the most vulnerable creatures, often without proper shelter or medical care. It breaks our hearts to see them suffer. But we can make a difference. Here are three ways we can help:

1. Build warm winter shelters. Imagine that these cats will have a cozy and safe place to call home during the freezing nights.

2. Take them for sterilization surgery. The thought of these cats reproducing and adding to the already overwhelming number of homeless felines is heart-wrenching. Let's do our part to control their population and ensure their safety.

3. Vaccinate against infectious diseases like FIV. These cats face numerous health risks living on the streets. By vaccinating them, we can help prevent the spread of diseases and keep them healthy.


Transparent & Trustworthy Donation

To ensure the authenticity of our actions that will truly benefit kitties, we take the following two ways to monitor the donated cash flow.

Bi-Weekly Kitty Videos for the Public

At our organization, we prioritize transparency in our donation process. We record videos of the kitties we help twice a month and post them online for public surveillance. This ensures that our donors can see where their money is going and have complete trust in the impact of their contributions.

Open Financial Reports

By making our financial reports open and accessible to the public, we ensure that our donors know exactly how their contributions are being used. This includes expenses for building shelters, purchasing food, and the total amount of funds we have raised.
